About

AI enthusiast, currently pursuing my Ph.D. and serving as a senior research fellow in the Department of Computer Science at Avinashilingam University, India. With over 3.5 years of research experience and 1 year of teaching experience in programming languages, I specialize in Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Data Analytics, the Internet of Things (IoT), Affective Computing (including multimodal data analysis), and Data Science. My research journey is marked by a strong commitment to conducting high-quality research that advances knowledge and addresses real-world challenges. I am deeply motivated to apply these core skills to various applications such as healthcare, education, human-computer interaction, and human-robotic interaction, solving complex problems. Additionally, I have published research articles in peer-reviewed and reputable international journals to support my dedication to continuous learning and research excellence. In addition, I had the opportunity to work on a patent named 'Kidbot 1.0,' where I designed a game based on a Mobile App to teach simple AI concepts to primary school children. Furthermore, I served as a mentor for postgraduate students' major projects.
